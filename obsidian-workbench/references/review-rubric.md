# Ingest Review Rubric

Use this rubric after creating or revising an ingest checklist.

## Combined Reviewer Lenses
Use one combined reviewer. The reviewer must check all three lenses in one pass:
- Truth lens: technically current, accurate, not misleading, not duplicated
- Use lens: operationally reusable, concrete procedures, evidence anchors, commands, links, proof standards, natural story flow
- Learning lens: understandable, explicit, unambiguous, arranged as a clear mental ladder

## What The Reviewer Should Flag
- Ambiguous wording that could imply the wrong actor, action, direction, causal relationship, proof boundary, or next step
- Missing reusable skill or note landing
- Backtick-wrapped wikilinks such as `` `[[lock_sock()]]` ``; they must be plain wikilinks so Obsidian renders them as links.
- Unclear proof standards or boundaries
- Raw-step duplication
- Weak actionability
- Missing future re-application value
- Weak recipe promotion
- Duplicate recipe-link fanout that is not required by checklist direct landing
- Story order that does not clearly follow logic sequence, time sequence, or easy-to-hard sequence
- Sections that still feel like pasted checkbox bundles instead of teaching pauses
